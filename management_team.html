The Management Team
Managing Director/CEO

Kanayo Eni-Ikeh

Kanayo Eni-Ikeh is the Managing Director / CEO at Atiat. He has built his career in finance managing strategic positions in Banking and Financial services. His vast experience has positioned him as a leader and prudent manager of resources Kanayo has over twelve years’ professional experience in the Financial Services Industry spanning across banking operations, retail, business and commercial banking. He has worked in various roles across reputable organizations such as Process Flow Outsourcing Limited, Diamond Bank, Fidelity Bank, VFD Group and VFD Microfinance Bank, where he successfully promoted the organization’s products and services to institutional and retail clients. Kanayo has attended various executive programs at The Lagos Business School; is an alumnus of The Indian School of Business and holds a B.Sc. in Geological Sciences from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka.
Chief Operating Officer

Otis Ehiemuan

Otis has 20 years hands-on experience in Treasury Management/ALM, Financial and Credit Risk Analysis, Financial Control, Strategic Planning and Capital Market Operations having worked in various capacity and units of AIICO Insurance Plc, IMB International Bank Plc/IMB Securities Plc, Stacoprime Capital Limited and Aquila Leasing Limited prior to joining Atiat Leasing Limited as the Chief Finance Officer/Chief Operating Officer. A graduate of Accounting, with MBA in Marketing. He is an Associate Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN)
Group Head, Business Development

Okechukwu Ndubisi

Okechukwu Ndubisi is an acknowledged leasing professional with core expertise in marketing, fleet management operations and sales. He started his career thirteen years ago at C & I Leasing Plc as a marketing executive and rose to the position of Head of Operation. He later joined Cadbury Nigeria Plc as a Sales Development Manager of the company. Prior to joining Atiat Leasing Limited, he worked as various heads; Head of Operation, Head of Marketing, Head of Outsourcing and logistics at Novo car rentals a subsidiary of Cashlink Leasing Plc. Okechukwu holds a Bachelor of Marketing (B.Sc.) degree from the University of Nigeria, Enugu Campus and Masters in Business Administration (MBA) from University of Lagos. He is an Associate of Chartered Institute of Marketing.
Head, Human Capital Management

She started her career in the Manufacturing industry where she was exposed to various aspects of Human Resources Practice. She has attended numerous courses in Human Resources Management. Kingsley Obinna Kanu

Kanu Kingsley Obinna, an avid salesman and relationship manager with over 14 years combined professional experience in sales, marketing and channel coordination across the leasing and telecoms industries. Obinna began his career in leasing with C & I Leasing Plc. where he worked for over four years. He has also worked in various leasing companies including Enterprise Leasing Limited and Premium Leasing Limited. Prior to joining Atiat Leasing Limited, Obinna rose to the position of Chief Operating Officer (COO) at Premium Leasing Limited. Obinna has a B.Sc. in Banking and Finance from Enugu State University of Science Technology (ESUT). He has attended numerous courses in both lease marketing and credit risk management.
